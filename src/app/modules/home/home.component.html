Clinical Applications

Vascular

Varicose veins occur when valves in the veins fail, leading to blood pooling and vessel enlargement.

Proctology

Proctological conditions such as hemorrhoids, anal fistula, and fissures cause pain, bleeding, and discomfort.

Gynecology

Laser technology is increasingly used in gynecological and cosmetic applications including vaginal tightening, rejuvenation.

PLDD

PLDD is a minimally invasive spinal procedure for herniated discs causing back pain or sciatica.

Aesthetic

Laser-assisted contouring and skin tightening stimulate collagen production and target localized fat deposits.

Body Lipolysis

Laser-induced selective heat melts stubborn fat that escapes through tiny access holes in the treated area.
