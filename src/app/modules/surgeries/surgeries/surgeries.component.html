Clinical Applications

VASCULAR SURGERY (EVLT)

Varicose veins occur when valves in the veins fail, leading to blood pooling and vessel enlargement.Endovenous Laser Treatment (EVLT) is a minimally invasive method that closes these veins using laser energy.

Laser Role:

A thin fiber is inserted into the vein under ultrasound guidance. The laser emits precise thermal energy (1470 nm / 980 nm) that seals the vein wall without affecting surrounding tissue.

Aesthetic / Dermatology

Laser-assisted contouring and skin tightening stimulate collagen production and target localized fat deposits.These treatments are popular in dermatology and cosmetic surgery clinics.

Laser Role:

Combined 1470 nm and 980 nm wavelengths enable selective targeting of water and hemoglobin,enhancing skin elasticity, improving texture, and reshaping body contours.

PLDD

PLDD is a minimally invasive spinal procedure for herniated discs causing back pain or sciatica.It reduces pressure inside the intervertebral disc by vaporizing a small portion of the nucleus pulposus.

Laser Role:

Using a fine optical fiber, the laser delivers energy through a needle under fluoroscopic guidance.The heat shrinks and decompresses the disc, relieving nerve pressure.

Proctology

Proctological conditions such as hemorrhoids, anal fistula, and fissures cause pain, bleeding, and discomfort.Traditional surgery often leads to long recovery times and postoperative complications.

Laser Role:

Laser energy is used for coagulation, vaporization, or excision of affected tissue with minimal bleeding and pain.It promotes faster healing and preserves healthy tissue.
